Evernote:

Evernote is a note book app, you can write text note on it, draw on it, and save photos on it, I use Evernote to keep track of my ideas, and it would sync my notes across all my devices.

Photoshop:

I use Photoshop for designing websites, I believe designing is the most important step in developing a website, and I rely on Photoshop to do it efficiently. Photoshop has a wide array of tools to help you align elements, mix and match colors and try out layouts. Using Photoshop to design websites can also hone your image editing skill which may become handy from time to time.

Webstorm:

Webstorm is an web development IDE, it packs the tools for project management, version control and live editing, it understands major frameworks such as bootstrap, angular, node.js, and can provide documents right away when you forget something. Webstorm is not a free software, but it is worth every penny, I would recommend anyone that does web development for a living to try it out.
